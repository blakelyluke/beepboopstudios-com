Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-05

This Privacy Policy describes how BeepBoop Studios LLC ("BeepBoop", "we", "us") collects, uses, and protects information about you when you use our products including the Level: Adult app and the website at leveladult.app.

What we collect

  • Account information — email address, username, first name, the avatar configuration you choose, and your selected major / professor in the app.
  • Usage data — quests completed, lessons finished, courses collected, streaks, XP, level, and other in-app progress needed for the gamification mechanics to work.
  • Device data — basic device identifiers (browser, OS) and a push-notification token if you enable notifications.
  • Subscription data — managed by Stripe (web), Apple App Store, or Google Play depending on platform. We never see or store your full payment card number.
  • Crash + diagnostic data — anonymized crash reports via Sentry to help us fix bugs.

How we use it

  • Operate the app — saving your progress, syncing across devices.
  • Send transactional notifications (e.g. quest reminders, when you opt in).
  • Identify and fix bugs.
  • Process subscription payments via the platforms above.

What we don't do

  • We don't sell your data.
  • We don't run advertising inside the app.
  • We don't share your personal data with third parties for their own marketing.

Service providers we use

  • Supabase — backend storage and authentication.
  • Stripe — web subscription processing.
  • Apple App Store / Google Play — mobile subscription processing.
  • Sentry — anonymous crash reporting.
  • Vercel — hosting.

Each of these handles your data under their own privacy policies, in line with industry-standard security and data-handling practices.

Account deletion

You can delete your account at any time from Settings → Delete Account inside the app. This permanently removes your profile, progress, and inventory from our database. Subscription cancellation, where applicable, is managed via Stripe, the App Store, or Google Play depending on where you purchased.

You can also request deletion by emailing support@leveladult.com.

Children

Level: Adult is intended for users 13 and older. We don't knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has signed up, please contact us and we'll delete the account.

Your rights (GDPR / CCPA)

If you're in the EU, UK, or California, you have the right to access, correct, or delete your data. Email support@leveladult.com and we'll respond within 30 days.

Changes

We may update this policy as the product evolves. Material changes will be notified in-app or by email. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will always reflect the current revision.

Contact
